Plot

In the beginning there were only great islands separated by an abyss. During the time of the great unification, the lands began to merge into one. After 1000 years, the islands united accompanied by great eruptions. A majestic Atol has emerged from the energy produced during the explosion. He became the lord of this world that he named Atolhir. His power allowed it to be transformed into the strength that unites the land. After 5000 years, when Atol began to age and came to the realisation that he no longer could take care of the world, he decided to created life. He created Islet, who was to take over his role, and Chiromantes, who took care of the 4 realms. However, Islet rebelled. He killed Atol and imprisoned Chiromantes. By this act, the lands began to separate again. That were a mysterious figure appears who's destined to save the Atolhir lands. Will he succeed?!


Gameplay

A fast paced 2D platform game with various mechanics diversifying the gameplay and an old school pixel art graphics. The charming story of this world will pull you in for hours of gameplay. Four diversified wolrd with their own unique mechanics and enemies will make you want to only play this game.
